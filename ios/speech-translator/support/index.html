About Speech Translator

Speech Translator is a powerful iOS app designed to help users translate speech and text between multiple languages. Whether you're traveling abroad, learning a new language, or communicating with people who speak different languages, our app provides instant and accurate translations.

Key Features:

  • Real-time speech-to-text translation
  • Support for multiple languages
  • Text-to-speech output for translated content
  • Offline translation mode (selected languages)
  • Clean and intuitive interface
  • Fast and accurate translation engine
  • Voice recognition with high accuracy
  • Copy and share translations easily
  • Translation history (stored locally)
  • Suitable for travelers and language learners
  • Privacy-focused - all processing on device
  • No account or registration required

Frequently Asked Questions

What languages are supported?

Speech Translator supports a wide range of languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and many more. The app continuously adds support for additional languages.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Once you download the necessary language packs, Speech Translator works completely offline. No internet connection is required for translations, making it perfect for international travel.

How accurate are the translations?

Speech Translator uses advanced translation algorithms to provide highly accurate translations. However, like all translation tools, accuracy can vary based on context, idioms, and language complexity.

Is my speech data stored or shared?

No! All speech recognition and translation processing happens entirely on your device. We do not store, collect, or share any audio recordings or translation data.

Can I save my translation history?

Yes! Translation history is saved locally on your device for quick reference. You can access, copy, and share previous translations anytime. All data stays on your device.

Does the app require microphone access?

Yes, to use the speech-to-text translation feature, the app requires microphone access. You can also use the text input mode if you prefer not to grant microphone permissions.

Do you have an Android version?

Currently, Speech Translator is available exclusively on iOS. An Android version is being considered based on user demand.

How do I download offline language packs?

You can download language packs from within the app settings. Select the languages you need, and they will be available for offline use after downloading.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please contact us with details about the issue, including your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots are also helpful!

Can I use this app for business purposes?

Yes! Speech Translator is perfect for business meetings, international calls, travel, and any situation where you need quick translations between languages.
