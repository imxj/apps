About Dashcam Loop

Dashcam Loop is a powerful iOS app designed to turn your iPhone into a reliable dashcam. Whether you're commuting, road tripping, or just want video evidence for peace of mind, our app provides continuous loop recording with automatic storage management.

Key Features:

  • Continuous loop recording
  • Automatic storage management
  • Background recording support
  • High-quality video capture
  • Clean and intuitive interface
  • Configurable recording duration
  • Quick access to saved clips
  • Easy video export and sharing
  • Low storage mode option
  • Suitable for all drivers
  • Privacy-focused - all data stays on device
  • No account or registration required

Frequently Asked Questions

How does loop recording work?

Dashcam Loop continuously records video and automatically overwrites the oldest footage when storage is full. This ensures you always have recent recordings without manually managing storage.

Does the app work in the background?

Yes! Dashcam Loop can continue recording even when you switch to other apps or lock your phone, ensuring uninterrupted footage during your drive.

How much storage does it use?

You can configure the maximum storage usage in the app settings. The app automatically manages space by removing old recordings when the limit is reached.

Is my video data stored or shared?

No! All video recordings are stored entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or share any of your footage or location data.

Can I save important clips?

Yes! You can mark clips as important to prevent them from being overwritten during loop recording. These clips will be preserved until you manually delete them.

Does the app require camera access?

Yes, camera access is required for video recording. The app uses your device's camera to capture dashcam footage.

Do you have an Android version?

Currently, Dashcam Loop is available exclusively on iOS. An Android version is being considered based on user demand.

What video quality options are available?

You can choose from multiple video quality settings to balance between video clarity and storage usage. Higher quality uses more storage per minute of recording.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please contact us with details about the issue, including your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots are also helpful!

Can I use this for commercial vehicles?

Yes! Dashcam Loop is perfect for personal vehicles, rideshare drivers, delivery drivers, and any situation where you need reliable video recording on the road.
