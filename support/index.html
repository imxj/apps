About Audio In Text Out InterviewAI

Audio In Text Out InterviewAI is a powerful iOS app that uses advanced artificial intelligence to convert your audio recordings into accurate, searchable text transcriptions. Whether you're conducting interviews, attending meetings, or recording lectures, our app makes it easy to capture and organize spoken content.

Key Features:

  • High-accuracy AI transcription technology
  • Support for multiple languages and accents
  • Real-time transcription during recording
  • Import existing audio files for transcription
  • Automatic speaker identification and labeling
  • Export transcriptions in multiple formats (TXT, PDF, DOCX)
  • Search and highlight within transcriptions
  • Time-stamped transcriptions for easy navigation
  • Offline processing for privacy and security
  • Cloud sync across your iOS devices
  • Professional formatting options
  • Audio playback synchronized with text

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the transcription?

Our AI transcription technology achieves industry-leading accuracy rates of 95%+ for clear audio recordings. Accuracy may vary based on audio quality, background noise, accents, and speaking pace.

What audio formats are supported?

The app supports most common audio formats including MP3, M4A, WAV, AIFF, and AAC. You can record directly in the app or import existing audio files from your device.

Is my audio data safe and private?

Absolutely. All transcription processing happens locally on your device - your audio never leaves your iPhone or iPad. We prioritize your privacy and don't store or access your recordings.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, Audio In Text Out InterviewAI works completely offline. No internet connection is required for recording or transcribing audio.

What languages are supported?

Currently, the app supports English with high accuracy. We're working on adding support for Spanish, French, German, and other major languages in future updates.

How long can my recordings be?

There's no strict limit on recording length. However, very long recordings (over 2 hours) may take longer to process and could impact device performance.

Do you have an Android version?

Currently, Audio In Text Out InterviewAI is available exclusively on iOS. An Android version is being considered based on user demand.

Can I export my transcriptions?

Yes! You can export transcriptions in multiple formats including plain text (.txt), PDF, and Microsoft Word (.docx). You can also share transcriptions directly via email or messaging apps.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please contact us with details about the issue, including your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots or screen recordings are also helpful!

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes! Audio In Text Out InterviewAI can be used for both personal and commercial purposes, including journalism, research, business meetings, and content creation.
