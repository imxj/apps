About Proper Casing Practice

Proper Casing Practice is an educational iOS app designed to help users master proper capitalization rules through interactive typing exercises. Whether you're a student learning grammar fundamentals or a professional looking to polish your writing skills, our app provides engaging practice sessions to improve your writing accuracy.

Key Features:

  • Interactive typing exercises with proper casing rules
  • Progressive difficulty levels from beginner to advanced
  • Real-time feedback on capitalization accuracy
  • Practice with sentences, paragraphs, and various text types
  • Grammar rule explanations and tips
  • Progress tracking and performance statistics
  • Custom practice sessions based on your weak areas
  • Offline practice mode - no internet required
  • Clean, distraction-free interface
  • Suitable for all ages and skill levels
  • Regular content updates with new exercises
  • Achievement system to motivate learning

Frequently Asked Questions

What capitalization rules does the app teach?

The app covers all major capitalization rules including proper nouns, sentence beginnings, titles, geographic locations, days/months, and more. Each exercise focuses on specific rules with clear explanations.

Is the app suitable for children?

Yes! Proper Casing Practice is designed for all ages and skill levels. The progressive difficulty system allows children to start with basic rules and advance at their own pace.

Can I track my progress?

Absolutely! The app includes detailed progress tracking, showing your accuracy rates, improvement over time, and areas that need more practice. You can view statistics for individual sessions and overall performance.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, Proper Casing Practice works completely offline. No internet connection is required for practice sessions, making it perfect for use anywhere.

How do the difficulty levels work?

The app features beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Beginners start with simple sentence capitalization, while advanced users practice complex texts with multiple capitalization rules applied simultaneously.

Can I customize my practice sessions?

Yes! The app allows you to focus on specific capitalization rules or areas where you need improvement. You can also adjust session length and difficulty to match your learning goals.

Do you have an Android version?

Currently, Proper Casing Practice is available exclusively on iOS. An Android version is being considered based on user demand.

How often is new content added?

We regularly update the app with new exercises, practice texts, and capitalization scenarios. Updates typically include fresh content every few weeks to keep your practice sessions engaging.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please contact us with details about the issue, including your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots are also helpful!

Can teachers use this app in classrooms?

Yes! Proper Casing Practice is perfect for educational settings. Teachers can use it for grammar lessons, homework assignments, or individual student practice to reinforce proper capitalization skills.
