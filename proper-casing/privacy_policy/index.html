1. What We Do

Proper Casing Practice is an educational app that helps you practice and improve your capitalization and grammar skills. The app provides interactive exercises and tracks your progress locally on your device.

2. What We Don't Store

We do not store, collect, or retain:

All practice exercises and progress tracking happen entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers or third-party services. The app works completely offline after installation.

All practice progress and settings are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage. You have complete control over this data and can reset your progress at any time within the app.

This app is intended for users 18 years and older. Users under 18 should have parental permission before using this app.
