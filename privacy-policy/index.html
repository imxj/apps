We do not store any personal information, audio recordings, transcriptions, or any data from your requests and responses. Your audio is processed by AI providers and results are returned directly to your device.

1. What We Do

Audio In Text Out InterviewAI uses AI providers (Google Gemini or OpenAI ChatGPT) to transcribe your audio recordings. Your audio is sent to these services for processing and the transcription is returned to your device.

2. What We Don't Store

We do not store, collect, or retain:

  • Your audio recordings
  • Your transcriptions
  • Any personal information
  • Any requests or responses to/from AI providers
  • Any data about you or your usage

3. AI Provider Processing

When you use the app, your audio is temporarily processed by third-party AI services. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing these policies to understand how your audio is handled during processing.

4. Your Data

All transcriptions are stored locally on your device. You have complete control to view or copy your transcriptions within the app.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
