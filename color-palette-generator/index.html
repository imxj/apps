Understanding Color Harmony

Color harmony refers to the property that certain aesthetically pleasing color combinations have. These combinations create pleasing contrasts and consonances that are said to be harmonious.

Complementary Colors

Colors directly opposite each other on the color wheel. Creates high contrast and vibrant looks.

Analogous Colors

Colors next to each other on the color wheel. Creates serene and comfortable designs.

Triadic Colors

Three colors evenly spaced around the color wheel. Offers strong visual contrast while retaining harmony.
