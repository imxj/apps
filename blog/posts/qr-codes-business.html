3 Customer Feedback & Reviews

Make it effortless for satisfied customers to leave reviews. Strategic QR code placement can dramatically increase your online reviews and provide valuable feedback.

Strategic Placement Ideas

  • Receipt QR Codes: Add to printed or digital receipts
  • Table Tents: Place on restaurant tables or waiting areas
  • Product Packaging: Include with shipped products
  • Service Completion: Share after appointments or services

  • Create a simple feedback form for quick responses

Best Practices for Business QR Codes

Design Guidelines:
  1. Size Matters: Minimum 2cm x 2cm for print materials
  2. Contrast is Key: Dark patterns on light backgrounds work best
  3. Test Everything: Scan from various distances and angles
  4. Add Context: Include a call-to-action like "Scan for Menu"
  5. Track Performance: Use URL shorteners with analytics
